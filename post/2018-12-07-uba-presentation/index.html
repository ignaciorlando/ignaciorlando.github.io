Retinal imaging talk at FCEN / UBA (Argentina)

I presented my work on machine learning for ophthalmic image analysis at University of Buenos Aires in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

José Ignacio Orlando

In December 7th I gave a lecture on machine learning for ophthalmic image analysis to the students of the “Deep Learning for Medical Image Analysis” course that Enzo Ferrante taught at the Computer Science Department from Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales, UBA. Thanks Enzo for the invitation!
