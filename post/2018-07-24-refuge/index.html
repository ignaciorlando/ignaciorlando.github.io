REFUGE: Retinal Fundus Glaucoma Challenge

I’m joining Yanwu Xu, Huazhu Fu and Hrvoje Bogunović as an organizer of REFUGE, the first retinal fundus glaucoma challenge at MICCAI 2018 in Granada, Spain.

José Ignacio Orlando
Jul 24, 2018 1 min read News, English
Project

This year I’ll be joining Yanwu Xu (Frank), Huazhu Fu and Hrvoje Bogunović as a co-organizer of REFUGE, the first MICCAI challenge on glaucoma detection in fundus images! It will be held as part of the Ophthalmic Medical Image Analysis (OMIA) workshop at MICCAI 2018 in Granada (Spain).

I’ll be responsible of grading the teams based on their submitted results, presenting the outcomes of the challenge and writing the summary publication. Thanks Hrvoje and Frank for your vote of confidence!

The deadline for submitting results on the REFUGE validation set either for glaucoma detection, optic disc/cup segmentation and/or fovea detection is this Friday 28th! Looking forward for your submissions!
