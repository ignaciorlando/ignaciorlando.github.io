I got an NVIDIA Hardware Grant

I was granted a Titan Xp for my research at OPTIMA by NVIDIA Corporation!

José Ignacio Orlando
Last updated on Mar 15, 2018 1 min read News, English, Grant

My application to the NVIDIA Hardware Grant was succesful! As part of this grant, I will receive a NVIDIA Titan XP GPU to perform my research on deep learning techniques for OCT image analysis. I thank NVIDIA Corporation for providing me with this cutting-edge hardware that will certainly help me to improve the efficiency of my methods.

Update 2028-03-15: I got the Titan Xp and I’m ready to put it on my computer!
